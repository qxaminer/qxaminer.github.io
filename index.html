About veroFields

Creative technologist exploring the intersection of AI and human creativity

veroFields is a creative technologist exploring the intersection of AI and human creativity. Currently pursuing a Masters in Creative Technology, with a focus on building AI systems that amplify rather than replace human artistic practice.

Current Projects

Developing an AI-powered color matching tool that analyzes photographs to recommend art supplies from an artist's existing collection—bridging digital inspiration with physical creation. This work reflects a core philosophy: technology should enhance creative expression, not overshadow it.

Research Focus

Research centers on bias mitigation in AI curation, multimodal systems, and "archaeological algorithms"—AI designed to surface overlooked cultural value rather than reinforce mainstream patterns. Through frameworks like "Politensioner analysis," the work explores how machine learning can democratize art discovery beyond traditional gatekeeping.

Vision

Creating AI systems that enhance human flourishing by augmenting creativity, facilitating meaningful collaboration, and preserving cultural nuance in our algorithmically-mediated world.
